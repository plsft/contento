Frequently asked

Can I start free and upgrade later?

Yes. The Starter plan is free with no credit card required. When you need more projects or pages, upgrade to Growth or Scale from the dashboard.

What happens if I exceed my page limit?

You will not be able to generate new pages until you upgrade or the next billing cycle. Existing pages remain live and accessible.

Is the AI generation included or do I need my own API key?

AI generation is included in all plans. The page limits on each plan reflect the total AI-generated pages you can create. No separate OpenAI or Anthropic API key is needed.

Can I use Contento for client projects?

Yes. The Scale plan supports unlimited projects and team seats, making it ideal for agencies managing multiple client domains.

Do I need to manage servers or infrastructure?

No. Contento is fully cloud-hosted. You install the CLI, create projects, and publish pages. We handle all hosting, SSL, CDN, and infrastructure.

Can AI agents use Contento?

Yes. Every CLI command supports a --json flag for machine-readable output. AI agents like Claude Code, Codex, and others can drive the entire pSEO workflow programmatically.
