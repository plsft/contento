Frequently asked

Why is it free?

Contento is a CLI, not a SaaS. The hard work — AI generation — is delegated to your provider. We don't host pages, we don't store your content, we don't run a server. There's nothing to charge for.

Do I need a Contento account?

No. There's no contento.sh login, no API key to provision, no dashboard. npm install -g contento, configure your AI provider key, and go.

Where does my data live?

On your machine. Generated pages, niche customizations, GSC analytics caches — everything sits in ~/.contento/ as plain JSON or SQLite. Easy to back up, easy to commit, easy to delete.

How are you funded?

Right now: GitHub sponsors and community contributions. We may offer paid niche packs or a hosted GSC aggregation service later, but the core CLI will stay free and open source.

Can I use it on client projects?

Yes. MIT license. Use it for your own sites, your clients' sites, your agency's whole portfolio. Same CLI, same AI provider key (or your client's), unlimited projects.

Can AI agents drive Contento?

Yes. Every command supports --json for machine-readable output and reads provider keys from env vars. Claude Code, Codex, and similar agents can run the entire pSEO workflow end-to-end without a human in the loop.
